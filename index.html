James Calo

MEng

James Calo recived his MEng in Computer Science from Imperial College London (thesis...). He is currently doing his PhD in Self-Supervised Federated Learning at the Hamlyn Centre, Imperial College London

Research Interests:

  • Improving the medical field with technologies such as Machine Learning
  • Low-energy embedded programming for IoT, especially IoMT
  • Federated Learning using Blockchain for decentralisation
  • Improving privacy for Federated Learning using Masked Autoencoders for vision
  • Bringing training to the Edge, allowing IoT devices to both train and run inference
  • Generative adversarial networks (GAN's) for generating training data for Machine Learning
